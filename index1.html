New Immigration Law

What is the Opportunity Card?

Your gateway to the German job market.

The Opportunity Card (Chancenkarte) is Germany's new residence permit under the Skilled Immigration Act. It allows qualified professionals to enter Germany without a job offer to search for employment for up to one year.

Background

Why Germany Introduced the Chancenkarte

Understanding the economic drivers behind the new law.

Germany is facing one of the largest skilled worker shortages in Europe, with over 1.9 million unfilled jobs across essential industries.

To stay competitive and support its growing economy, Germany needs qualified professionals from around the world, particularly from rapidly developing countries like India, Philippines, Vietnam, Indonesia, Brazil, and UAE.

The Opportunity Card was introduced to make immigration simpler, faster, and more transparent for global talent.
