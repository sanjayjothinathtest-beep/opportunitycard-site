What is the Opportunity Card?

The Opportunity Card (Chancenkarte) is Germany's new residence permit under the Skilled Immigration Act (¬ß20a AufenthG). It represents a paradigm shift in German immigration policy.

Unlike traditional visas, it allows qualified professionals from countries like India to enter Germany without a job offer and look for employment for up to one year.
